Enrol to Vote

You are eligible to vote if you're now 18 or you will be 18 on the next polling day. The next Queensland election is likely to be in March, 2015, but it could be much earlier than that. If you will turn 18 between now and March, you should enrol today so that you will be eligible to vote from your next birthday onward.

Not sure if you are enrolled? Check your enrolment online.

Not enrolled? Click here to enrol online via the Australian Electoral Commission website.

You can also use the Check your enrolment facility to verify whether you're enrolled in the seat (the "state district") of Noosa.
